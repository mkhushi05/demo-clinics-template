Meet Elena Davies, Founder & Lead Practitioner

Welcome to Lumière Aesthetics Clinic. I founded this clinic with a singular vision: to raise the standard of care in the aesthetics industry and provide a safe space where patients can achieve beautiful, undetectable results.

With over 8 years of clinical experience in both the NHS and private aesthetic sector, my approach is fundamentally anchored in medical safety, detailed anatomical knowledge, and an artistic eye for facial proportions.

My goal is never to change how you look, but to restore confidence and help you feel like the best version of yourself.
