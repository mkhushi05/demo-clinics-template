Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2026

At Lumière Aesthetics Clinic, we take your privacy and the security of your personal and medical information very seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with safe and effective medical aesthetic treatments. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Name, date of birth, photographic ID (for age verification).
  • Contact Data: Address, email address, telephone number.
  • Medical & Health Data (Special Category Data): Detailed medical history, current medications, allergies, previous aesthetic treatments, and clinical photographs (Before & Afters) for your medical file.
  • Financial Data: Payment details (processed securely via our third-party payment provider; we do not store full card details).

2. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we use it to:

  • Safely assess your suitability for medical aesthetic treatments.
  • Maintain accurate clinical records as required by our governing bodies and insurance providers.
  • Process bookings, deposits, and send appointment reminders.
  • Respond to your enquiries and manage aftercare.

3. Clinical Photography

As a medical aesthetic clinic, we are required to take clinical 'Before and After' photographs to document your baseline and treatment outcome. These are kept strictly confidential in your medical file. We will never use your photographs for marketing or social media purposes without obtaining separate, explicit written consent from you.

4. Data Security & Storage

We use robust, encrypted clinical software to store your medical records. Access is strictly restricted to authorised medical personnel. We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorised way.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights including the right to request access to your personal data, request correction of your data, or request erasure of your data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@lumiereclinic.co.uk.
