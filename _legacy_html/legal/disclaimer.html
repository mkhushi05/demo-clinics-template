Medical Disclaimer

In the event of a medical emergency following a treatment outside of clinic hours, please contact 111, attend your nearest A&E, and notify the clinic as soon as possible via our emergency contact lines provided at treatment.

The content provided on the Lumière Aesthetics Clinic website (including text, graphics, images, and other material) is for informational and educational purposes only. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

1. No Guaranteed Outcomes

Aesthetic treatments are medical procedures, and individual results will vary. The 'Before and After' photographs displayed on this website are of actual Lumière Clinic patients, but they do not guarantee that your results will be identical. Healing times, longevity of results, and potential side effects differ from person to person.

2. Prescription Medications

Certain treatments discussed on this website, such as botulinum toxin (anti-wrinkle injections), involve the use of Prescription Only Medicines (POM). These cannot be prescribed without a comprehensive face-to-face consultation with our prescribing medical professional to determine clinical suitability.

3. Risks and Complications

All medical and aesthetic procedures, including dermal fillers and anti-wrinkle injections, carry inherent risks. While we follow rigorous medical protocols to minimise these risks, complications (such as bruising, swelling, asymmetry, infection, or vascular occlusion) can occur. Fully informed written consent detailing these risks will be obtained before any procedure is carried out.
