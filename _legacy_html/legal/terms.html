Lumière Aesthetics Clinic reserves the right to refuse treatment if our medical practitioner deems it inappropriate, unsafe, or not in your best psychological or physical interests. We strictly will not treat anyone under the age of 18.

3. Results and Expectations

Aesthetic medicine is an art as well as a science, and results cannot be fully guaranteed. Every individual's anatomy and metabolism vary. While we strive for absolute client satisfaction, we cannot offer refunds for aesthetic outcomes.

4. Medical History

It is the client's responsibility to provide an honest, full, and accurate medical history during the consultation. This includes disclosing all medications, allergies, and previous aesthetic treatments. Withholding medical information can severely compromise your safety and treatment outcome.
