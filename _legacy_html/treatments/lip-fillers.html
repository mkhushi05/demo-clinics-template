The Lumière Approach to Lip Fillers

At Lumière Aesthetics Clinic in Manchester, we believe lip enhancements should whisper, not shout. Our medically qualified practitioner, Elena Davies, uses advanced techniques to enhance your lips' natural shape, restore lost volume, and provide deep hydration without an artificial 'overfilled' appearance.

We use only premium, temporary hyaluronic acid (HA) dermal fillers. Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring substance in the body, making it exceptionally safe and highly effective at retaining moisture for a soft, pillowy texture.

What to Expect: Your Journey

1. In-Depth Consultation

Every treatment begins with a thorough medical consultation. We discuss your aesthetic goals, assess your facial proportions, and determine if lip fillers are the right option for you. We take a 'less is more' approach, often recommending 0.5ml to 1ml for new patients.

2. The Procedure

Your comfort is paramount. We apply a strong topical numbing cream prior to treatment, and our premium fillers contain Lidocaine (a local anaesthetic) to minimise discomfort. The procedure takes approximately 30 minutes, using either a fine needle or a micro-cannula for safety and precision.

3. Results & Longevity

Results are visible immediately, though minor swelling and potential bruising may obscure the final look for 3 to 7 days. Once settled, your natural lip fillers will typically last between 6 to 12 months, depending on your metabolism and lifestyle.

Safety & Aftercare

As a medically-led clinic, safety is our priority. In the rare event of a complication, our clinic is fully equipped with hyaluronidase (the reversing agent for HA fillers) and our practitioner is trained in emergency complications management.

Immediate Aftercare:

  • Avoid touching or rubbing the area for 12 hours.
  • No strenuous exercise, saunas, or steam rooms for 24-48 hours.
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours.
  • Apply arnica cream if bruising occurs.
