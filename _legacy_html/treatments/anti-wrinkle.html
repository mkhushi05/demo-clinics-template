Anti-Wrinkle Injections in Manchester

Discreet, precise, and highly effective. Relax facial muscles to smooth away lines while maintaining natural expression and movement.

The Secret to Ageing Gracefully

At Lumière Aesthetics Clinic in Manchester, our anti-wrinkle injections offer a non-surgical solution to combating signs of ageing. We use premium formulation Botulinum Toxin to temporarily relax the specific facial muscles responsible for creating dynamic lines and wrinkles.

Our goal is never a "frozen" look. We specifically dose and target treatments to soften lines while preserving your ability to freely express emotion, resulting in a refreshed, well-rested appearance.

Common Areas Treated

Anti-wrinkle injections are highly versatile and can treat a variety of concerns in the upper face.

Frown Lines (Glabella)

Smooths the vertical '11' lines between the eyebrows that can cause an angry or tired expression.

Forehead Lines

Softens the horizontal creases across the upper forehead caused by raising eyebrows.

Crow's Feet

Reduces the fine lines that fan out from the outer corners of the eyes when smiling.

Preventative Ageing

By relaxing muscles early, it prevents fine lines from deepening into permanent static wrinkles.

Medical Disclaimer: Prescription Only

Important: Botulinum Toxin is a Prescription Only Medicine (POM). We strictly adhere to medical guidelines requiring a face-to-face consultation prior to any treatment to establish suitability. We cannot legally advertise brand names (e.g., Botox®) as services direct to the public.

Aftercare Information

  • Remain upright for 4 hours post-treatment.
  • Do not rub or massage the treated areas for 24 hours to prevent product migration.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, intense heat (saunas/sunbeds), and alcohol for 24-48 hours.
  • Avoid facial treatments or chemical peels for 14 days.
